Why Information Assurance & Security Matters

In the digital age, information is one of the most valuable organizational assets. Protecting it is no longer just an IT concern ‚Äî it‚Äôs a critical business, legal, and ethical responsibility. Information Assurance (IA) ensures that information remains confidential, integrity, and available to authorized users, while Information Security (InfoSec) focuses on safeguarding it from threats, vulnerabilities, and attacks.

Whitman & Mattord emphasize that successful security programs require both due care and due diligence: the proactive planning to protect information and the continuous oversight to ensure protections remain effective.

With cyber threats growing in sophistication, a well-structured security approach combines people, processes, and technology. This integrated defense safeguards against breaches, ensures business continuity, complies with laws, and maintains trust with stakeholders.
